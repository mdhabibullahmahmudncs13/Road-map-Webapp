About the Event

NASA Space Apps Challenge is an international mass collaboration focused on space exploration that takes place over 48 hours in cities around the world. NASA is going to organize this global event with the Bangladesh Association of Software and Information Services (BASIS) as the local organizer for the event in Bangladesh.

This mega event will be solely supported by BASIS Student's Forum. BASIS is the core organizer of the International Space Apps Challenge 2025 across Bangladesh where Dhaka, Chattogram, Sylhet, Rajshahi, Rangpur, Barisal, Khulna, Cumilla & Mymensingh will be the participating cities.
